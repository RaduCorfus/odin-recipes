Tuscan Chicken Soup

Description

Tuscan soup, also known in Italy as minesta di pane (bread soup), is a soup from the region of Tuscany, northern Italy.

This recipe will show you an easy way to bring this piece of traditional Italian cousine into your home.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1/2 small onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 containers chicken bone broth
  • 3/4 teaspoon Tuscan seasoning
  • 1 skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 1 handful roughly chopped spinach
  • 2 tablespoons sun dried tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup high protein pasta
  • 2 tablespoons finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Add oil to a saucepan over medium heat. Once oil is hot, add onion, and saut√© until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic; saute until fragrant; about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in chicken bone broth. Add Tuscan seasoning, chicken pieces, sun-dried tomatoes, and torn spinach. Stir to combine. Add in the pasta. Simmer until pasta is tender with a bite, about 10 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle in Parmesan; stir to combine. Stir in heavy cream. Serve immediately.
